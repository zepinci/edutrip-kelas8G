Let me show you my mindmap about Taipei 101! As shown in the picture, we were asked to make 10 different questions we had in mind about our selected destination. I chose Taipei 101 because it is the most iconic building in the whole city of Taipei. Not only that, the questions must be answered by personal experience. So, I wrote and answered several questions like "Is Taipei 101 really worth visiting?" and "How does it feel riding the world's fastest elevator?" The information gathered in this mindmap will act as a big picture of what the information inside our travel writing will look like.
